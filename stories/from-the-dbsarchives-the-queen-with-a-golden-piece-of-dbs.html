From the #DBSArchives: The Queen with a golden piece of DBS

22 Sep 2022

Our pioneering spirit

Singapore Parliament observed one minute of silence on 12 September 2022 as a mark of respect to Queen Elizabeth II. On her tribute to Her Late Majesty, Leader of the House, Ms. Indranee Rajah, shared that the Rollei factory in Kampong Chai Chee was one of the places visited by the Queen during her first State Visit to Singapore in 1972.

Rollei was one of the first companies DBS invested in when the Singapore government successfully brought them in to industrialise the economy in 1971. "Made by Rollei Singapore" was proudly engraved on the cameras they made.

We came across this photo of President Benjamin Sheares presenting gifts to Her Majesty at the Istana State Room, where one of the gifts was a gold-plated Rollei 35 camera.

Her Majesty has been seen using the same gold-plated Rollei 35 camera on several occasions to take pictures years later. Some were dated ten years after she received the camera!
