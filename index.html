Live more by the Bay

DBS Live more by the Bay aims to enliven and transform the Marina Bay area into a vibrant space that brings the community together to connect, discover, and engage in meaningful experiences. We also seek to enable the underserved to enjoy opportunities that they may not be able to otherwise, and foster a more inclusive society.

Partnerships

DBS is committed to uplifting lives and livelihoods in the community, and bringing others onboard this shared journey towards a better and more inclusive future. With the support of like minded partners, DBS Live more by the Bay seeks to be a force for good that drives and enables positive change from the very heart of Singapore.
