DBS Perspectives @ Esplanade

Innovative & Sustainable Garden City

Venue: DBS Foundation Outdoor Theatre, Esplanade

Time: 6:30pm - 8:00pm

Hosted by Vernatta Lopez, discover innovative gardening and sustainable living solutions presented by garden gurus and the next young generation of impact makers on stage. Be prepared to be immersed in interactive screenings and a live demonstration of a Succulent Bouquet making workshop, led by the garden gurus themselves!

That’s not all! Stand to win prizes when you take our quiz and give the correct answers! Join us as we learn more about how we can play a part in strengthening Singapore’s food security and resilience through easy and fun gardening methods.
